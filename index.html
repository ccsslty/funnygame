Basic ZOO BOOM Mechanics

Matching Animals

In ZOO BOOM, you need to connect three or more identical animals to remove them from the board. Unlike traditional match-3 games, ZOO BOOM allows you to connect animals in any direction – horizontally, vertically, and diagonally – as long as they are the same type and adjacent to each other.

Creating Chains

The longer the chain of matching animals you create in ZOO BOOM, the higher your score. Try to connect as many animals as possible in a single move to maximize points and progress through levels faster. ZOO BOOM rewards strategic thinking and pattern recognition.

Level Objectives

Each level in ZOO BOOM has specific objectives that must be completed to progress. These might include:

  • Collecting a certain number of specific animals
  • Clearing obstacles from the board
  • Reaching a target score within a limited number of moves
  • Bringing special items to the bottom of the board
  • Defeating boss characters by matching specific patterns

Pay attention to the objective display at the beginning of each ZOO BOOM level to understand what you need to accomplish.

Advanced ZOO BOOM Techniques

Power-ups and Special Items

As you progress through ZOO BOOM, you'll unlock various power-ups that can help you overcome challenging board layouts. These special items are created by matching animals in specific patterns:

Line Blaster

Match 4 animals in a line to create a Line Blaster in ZOO BOOM. When activated, this power-up clears an entire row or column, depending on the direction of your swipe.

Area Bomb

Create an L or T shape match in ZOO BOOM to generate an Area Bomb. This powerful item clears all animals in a 3×3 area around it when activated.

Animal Collector

Match 5 or more animals in ZOO BOOM to create an Animal Collector. When used, this special item removes all animals of the same type from the entire board.

Super Combo

Combine two power-ups in ZOO BOOM to create devastating effects that can clear large portions of the board. Experiment with different combinations to discover all possible super combos.

Mastering these power-ups and learning when to use them is key to becoming a ZOO BOOM expert and tackling the most challenging levels in the game.
